About

Players battle to defeat their opponent's Pharaoh while also defending their own. The first team to defeat the opponent's Pharaoh will win! Cast rituals to summon resources, status effects, and enchantments. Lead your team to victory and overcome Adversity!

Originally created by MrScaryMuffin and T3hMuffinz, inspired from Calamity by Moesh, and remastered by Jeven, this latest edition offers modern game play for teams of 2 (4 players) or 3 (6 players).
